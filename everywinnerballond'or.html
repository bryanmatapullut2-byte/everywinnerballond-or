BALLON D'OR WINNER


2025 MALE WINNER - OUSMANE DEMBÉLÉ



In a historic moment for French soccer, Ousmane Dembélé has been crowned the winner of the Ballon d’Or 2025,
edging out fierce competition at the prestigious ceremony held in Paris on September 22, 2025.


2025 FEMALE WINNER - AITANA BONMATÍ


Celebrated Barcelona and Spain midfielder Aitana Bonmatí took home her third consecutive Ballon d'Or at Monday's 2025 awards,
becoming the first-ever women's footballer to accomplish the feat.


2024 MALE AND FEMALE WINNER - RODRI & AITANA BONMATÍ



The winner of the 2024 Ballon d'Or is Rodri,
who claimed the award after an exceptional season with Manchester City and the Spanish national team.
The 2024 Women's Ballon d'Or winner is Aitana Bonmatí,
who won the award for the second consecutive year.
She finished ahead of her teammates Caroline Graham Hansen and Salma Paralluelo, who completed the podium places.


2023 MALE & FEMALE WINNER - LIONEL MESSI & AITANA BONMATÍ


Lionel Messi and Aitana Bonmati have been named the 2023 Ballon d’Or winners on Monday
(30 October) at a star-studded ceremony in Paris.


2022 MALE & FEMALE WINNER - KARIM BENZEMA & ALEXIA PUTELLAS



The full Ballon d'Or 2022 rankings crowning the world's best footballers have been announced,
with Karim Benzema and Alexia Putellas the big winners


2021 MALE & FEMALE - LIONEL MESSI & ALEXIA PUTELLAS



Alexia Putellas and Lionel Messi have been crowned winners of the 2021 Ballon d’Or
during a glittering ceremony in Paris.


2020 MALE & FEMALE


The 2020 Ballon d'Or rankings were not officially awarded due to the COVID-19 pandemic,
but several players were considered top contenders:
Robert Lewandowski (Bayern Munich) was widely regarded as the frontrunner for the award.
1 Lionel Messi (Barcelona) was the current holder and consistently ranked among the top players.
1 Cristiano Ronaldo (Juventus) was also in the discussions for the top rankings.
1 Other notable contenders included players like Kevin De Bruyne and Neymar, who were highlighted in various power rankings.
2 While there was no official winner, these players dominated discussions around the 2020 Ballon d'Or.


2019 MALE & FEMALE WINNER -LIONEL MESSI & MEGAN RAPINCE




2018 MALE & FEMALE WINNER - LUKA MODRIC & ADA HEGERBERG


In 2018, Luka Modrić won the men's Ballon d'Or,
ending a decade of Messi/Ronaldo dominance, while Norwegian striker Ada Hegerberg won the inaugural Ballon d'Or Féminin (Women's Ballon d'Or).
Both were significant firsts for the prestigious awards presented by France Football.


2017 MALE WINNER - CRISTIANO RONALDO


Cristiano Ronaldo won the award for the fifth time on 7 December 2017,
equalling the highest tally of Ballons d'Or in history at the time,
sharing the record with Lionel Messi.


2016 MALE WINNER - CRISTIANO RONALDO


Ronaldo scored 745 points out of a maximum possible of 865.
It was his fourth Ballon d'Or,
the most of any European player in the history of the award.
